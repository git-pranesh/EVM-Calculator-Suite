Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the EVM Calculator handles information. The short version: it does not collect any personal information, because all calculations happen locally in your browser.

What we collect

Nothing personal. The site does not require an account, does not request your name, email, or any other identifying information, and does not run third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

Inputs and calculations

The numeric values you enter for Budget at Completion, Planned Value, Earned Value, and Actual Cost remain in your browser. They are processed in JavaScript on your device and are never transmitted to any server operated by this project.

Cookies

The calculator does not set its own cookies. The hosting provider may set strictly necessary cookies for security and routing. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Third-party requests

The page requests fonts from a public CDN to render typography. No other third-party services are loaded by the calculator.

Children's privacy

The site is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

Changes to this policy will be posted on this page. The effective date will be updated whenever a substantive change is made.
